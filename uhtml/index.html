  • F: Gravitational Force, in N

  • m1: Mass of Object 1, in Kg

  • m2: Mass of Object 2, in Kg

  • d: Distance between the two Objects, in m

  • G: Gravitation Constant, is 6.67 × 10-11 N.m2/kg2
